WaterPass Class

Extends Pass
Defined in: src\passes\refraction.js:5
Module: solution

The water pass renders the reflection and refraction of the given scene to a texture which is then used by a water material during the normal rendering process. The material's time value is updated automatically.

You may disable the water material if you want to use the generated textures for something else.

Add this pass to an EffectComposer and follow it up with a RenderPass.

Manipulate the water mesh however you like. You may also add the water material to other objects.
